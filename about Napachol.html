About Me

My Story

I am Napachol, a UI designer with a background in fine art, video editing, illustration, and marketing automation. Before bringing myself into UI design world, I’ve worked as a creative in a television production squad, shifted to be part of a marketing and communication team, and then challenged myself by joining the marketing automation sector.

My story began as a junior marketing automation specialist. My role was to create the assets within marketing automation tools, which sometime needed basic frontend development knowledge to create or make changes to the design. With little experience in IT and tech skills, the tasks at first were deemed as puzzling but over time it became much clearer. Having gotten a taste for what it’s like, I finally discovered my dream career as a UI designer / Frontend developer.

As a designer, my goal is to combine a passion of art with my interest in design innovation for the purpose of building attractive products focusing on user-centered design. With many years working with various design projects and dealing with different requirements has taught me to capture the needs of clients and work together in a team to accomplish goals.

I welcome the challenge and want to be a part of a compassionate and supportive environment where I can grow as a UI designer.
